Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our food and travel blog, content creation tips, and more.

What is this template about?
This is a modern, responsive Food & Travel Blog HTML template designed for travelers, food enthusiasts, bloggers, and content creators.
Is this a WordPress theme?
No, this is a static HTML template. You can convert it to WordPress or any CMS later if needed.
Can I customize the design and colors?
Yes! All colors, fonts, images, and layout sections are fully customizable using simple HTML/CSS files.
Is this template mobile-friendly?
Absolutely — the template is fully responsive and works smoothly on all devices, including phones, tablets, and large desktops.
Are images included in the download?
Demo images are for preview purposes only. Placeholder images are included in the download package.
What travel destinations do you cover?
We cover a wide range of destinations from popular tourist spots to hidden gems. Our focus is on authentic experiences, local cuisine, and sustainable travel practices.
How often is new content published?
We publish new articles every week, featuring travel guides, restaurant reviews, recipes, and photography tips. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated!
Can I contribute to your blog?
Yes, we welcome guest contributors who share our passion for food and travel. Please check our contributor guidelines and submit your pitch through our contact form.
